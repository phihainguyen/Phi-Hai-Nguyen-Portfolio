WHO IS PHI-HAI NGUYEN?

A health profesional turned coding enthusiast. Phi-Hai Nguyen is a person who loves helping people. Phi Hai discovered that by coding he can help make people's lives easier.

He realized with web development he is also able to express a side to who he is, which is his artistic side, something that wasn't possible in his previous profession. He displays his creativity by trying to create beautiful websites which he hope others can appreciate and that it may be seens as simply a website that people would enjoy visiting.
